You are walking in the forest at night. The shadows of trees among the starry night sky are the only things that guide you in this deep, dark place.
While you walk down the path of trees, you hear the sound of bushes rustling, and turn towards the source of the sound. You are frozen with fear;what could possibly be there? The sounds grow louder, and the leaves fly out the bush as whatever's in there rips apart the branches within them. Soon enough, the creature emerges from the bush, walking on all fours, and sniffing around for its next prey. You can't make out what it is, but once it stands up on its legs and emits a deep growl, you see the shadowy sillouette of a bear.
It would be wise to make a decision before it spots you.
